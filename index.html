I am currently pursuing an undergraduate BS degree in Business Management with a minor in Leadership. My dream job is one where I can solve problems in Programming, and be a leader in the company. Being a leader on campus have shown me many different ways and opportunities that one can be a leader and have a positive influence on their environment. The highest leadership position at the University of South Florida is being an Ambassador. In this position we are the &qout;Pride and Spirit of USF&qout; and work with the university president, Dr. Genshaft, as well as many donors. Working with the university president and meeting donors of my university have been a very humbling opportunity for me. All of these opportunities have changed my view on leadership from being a role assigned to one individual to a set of actions, performed by any individual that promotes an environment in which people can be themselves. Fostering kind feelings, growth and development, individuality, and a non-hostile environment is what leaders should strive to always accomplish.
